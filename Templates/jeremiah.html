Ciao Ciao!

Hi, my name is Ciao Ciao and I am a Blue Rose-Ringed Parakeet!

I am 2 years old, and for my species, if under the care of bird enthusiests like Jeremiah's Father in Law, my friends and I can live a long life between 20 to 30 years! At home, my diet includes seeds that are specifically blended for my robust diet with a healthy rotation of fruits native to Asia similarly to what we would eat in the wild.

I enjoy whistling, climbing, but most of all talking. One of my favorite phrases to say is, "What's up bud!" and "Whatchu doin?". Sometimes I even scare my family by imitating the smoke detector alarm after an intense dinner preparation. It tends to add to the chaos.

I enjoy being a part of my family and I know they love me too. If you'd like to hear me whitsle, watch my video!
